About HR Gladiator

Human Resources
Built to Win.

Founded and led by Whitney Brewton, Executive Director, HR Gladiator LLC is a full-service human resources consultancy based in Grand Blanc, Michigan. We partner with businesses of every size—locally and nationally—to design HR frameworks that protect, develop, and activate their most valuable asset: people.

Whether you're a startup building your first HR infrastructure or an established company navigating complex compliance landscapes, we bring the strategy, systems, and hands-on expertise to champion your workforce.

What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive HR solutions delivered by a seasoned professional — available to organizations wherever they operate.

Leadership Development

Building confident, capable leaders at every tier of your organization through targeted coaching and structured development programs.

Risk Management

Identifying and neutralizing workforce liabilities before they escalate—protecting your organization's culture, reputation, and bottom line.

HR Policy & Procedures

Crafting clear, enforceable policies and employee handbooks that set expectations, reduce disputes, and keep your workplace legally sound.

HR Software Systems

Evaluating, implementing, and optimizing HRIS platforms so your people data works for you — not against you.

Recruitment

End-to-end talent acquisition strategy: job architecture, sourcing, screening, and onboarding designed to hire right the first time.

Employee Grievance Management

Structured, impartial processes to resolve workplace conflicts swiftly and fairly — preserving trust and maintaining legal compliance.

HR Compliance

Auditing and aligning your HR practices to federal, state, and local employment law — staying ahead of regulatory changes so you don't have to.

Employee Engagement
